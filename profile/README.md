## Profile

*{protocell:labs}* is a digital laboratory merging artistic and research practice founded in 2021 by [Luka](https://twitter.com/LukaPiskorec) and [Kane](https://twitter.com/00000Kane00000), two computational designers from the far north. We investigate computational structures, mathematical forms of organization, design algorithms, artificial biology, emergent morphogenesis and digital graphics.
